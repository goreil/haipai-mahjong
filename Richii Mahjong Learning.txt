# Defense

## 1. Notice Defense possibility
1. Richii
2. Open Hand
3. Dama -> Do not defend against dama (closed hand no richii), happens rarely

### Open Hand is ready when 
1. He has three or more open sets / runs.
2. When he is doing a flush hand, he starts discarding tiles in
the suit he is supposedly collecting.
3. He keeps discarding the tile that he draws.

### Open hand is expensive when
1. Count dora

### Defense
Against toitoi -> Check board for played

chii + discard a tile in the same suit -> the neighborhood of the last discard is safe
chii + discard a tile in a different suit -> the neighborhood of the last discard is dangerous
pon -> wait can be anything



## 2. Decision

Push if two of the following conditions are met, fold otherwise
1. Ready hand;
2. High scoring hand: 7700 score or more
3. Good wait.
A) Tenpai -> side wait, semi side-wait (AABC), ABCD (waiting for pair)
B) 1-shanten -> only when guaranteed good wait

## 3. How to defend
1. 100% Safe tiles
- Genbutsu (discarded tiles)
2. 4th Honor
3. Suji
Notice: Remember to take into account tile blockate (kabe)
